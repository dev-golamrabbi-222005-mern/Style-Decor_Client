Project Name: Style Decor

Purpose: The primary purpose of StyleDecor is to modernize and streamline the operations of a local decoration company by solving common issues related to client flow, booking management, and service coordination. It aims to eliminate long waiting times, replace manual booking processes, and efficiently manage the allocation of specialized decorators for both consultations and on-site services.

Live URL: https://style-decor-44738.web.app

Key features:
For Booking & Scheduling -   Smart appointment scheduling for consultations. Allows users to explore packages, check decorator availability, and select a date/time.
For Service Management -	Efficient management of multiple decorators, their specialties, and on-site service assignments and coordination.
Client Interaction -	Users can choose their service mode (in-studio or on-site) and receive real-time project status updates.
Financial -    Integrated payment system for booking packages and services.
Business Insights -    Provides a powerful dashboard and analytics for business management and insights.
